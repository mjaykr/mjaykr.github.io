About Me

I am a dedicated and skilled Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Sheffield, with a strong focus on the mechanical properties of battery materials. My work within the FutureCAT consortium involves pioneering research into the fabrication and characterization of complex metal oxides for next-generation energy storage.

With a Ph.D. from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, my expertise spans advanced material characterization techniques, including in-situ nano-indentation and micro-computed tomography, complemented by a deep understanding of crystal plasticity modeling. My goal is to contribute to the development of more resilient and efficient materials to address critical engineering challenges.
